Problem Statement

Breaking the silence between two worlds.

Over 63 million people in India have significant hearing loss. The communication gap between the deaf and hearing communities creates barriers in education, employment, and daily life.

Existing solutions require trained interpreters who are scarce and expensive. SignSense AI provides an always-available, real-time, machine-learning-based bridge — using only a standard webcam.

Indian Sign Language

ISL is a complete, natural language with its own grammar, syntax, and vocabulary — distinct from British or American Sign Language.

Recognized as an official language of India
Dual-hand articulation with body posture cues
Regional variations across Indian states
Heavily used in education, courts, healthcare

System Pipeline

From webcam frame to predicted gesture — in milliseconds.

📷
STEP 01

Video Capture

OpenCV captures live webcam frames at 30 fps and mirrors the image

🦴
STEP 02

Keypoint Extraction

MediaPipe extracts 33 pose + 21+21 hand landmarks = 258D feature vector

🔧
STEP 03

Normalization

Spatial normalization relative to hip center & shoulder scale removes camera bias

📈
STEP 04

EMA Smoothing

Exponential Moving Average filter (α=0.5) reduces landmark jitter between frames

🧠
STEP 05

LSTM Inference

30-frame sequence fed into 2-layer LSTM → Softmax over 49 gesture classes

🗳️
STEP 06

Voting Buffer

20-frame majority voting (12/20 threshold) ensures stable, flicker-free UI output

Key Capabilities

Engineered for accuracy, speed, and robustness.

🎯

High-Confidence Thresholding

Only predictions with confidence > 85% are accepted. This prevents false positives during ambiguous or transitional gestures.

🔄

Majority Voting Buffer

A 20-frame rolling buffer with a 12-vote majority threshold eliminates UI flicker and provides stable, human-readable output.

📊

Motion Variance Check

An idle-state detector (variance < 0.001) suppresses inference when no meaningful gesture movement is detected, saving computation.

📍

Spatial Normalization

All keypoints are normalized relative to hip center and shoulder distance, making the model invariant to user distance and camera angle.

〰️

EMA Temporal Smoothing

Exponential Moving Average with α=0.5 smooths landmark positions across frames, reducing noise without introducing lag.

⚡

GPU / CPU Adaptive

Automatically runs on CUDA GPU if available, else falls back to CPU — ensuring compatibility across all hardware configurations.

    input_size=258,
    hidden_size=128,
    num_layers=2,
    num_classes=49,
    dropout=0.5):
